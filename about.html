About

Spicyo is an award winning restaurant featuring the finest cuts of organic meats, fresh food and variety of vegetarian options paired with premium drinks in a warm contemporary African themed setting.
The restaurant features both indoors and outdoors seating to suit different dining experiences including intimate dining with friends and family for special anniversaries and celebrations, corporate networking and groups.

Best Food

The Chef’s table experience gets you up close and personal with our engaging culinary team. Lovers of contemporary dining will delight in selecting their preferred spot, menu and interacting with the chef as he creates different dishes from the day’s best ingredients and personally presenting each course. For an intimate experience of up to 4 guests, a table will full view of the kitchen is available while large groups can share the experience in the patio next to the herb garden.Every Friday, indulge in our amazing whisky pairing dinner prepared with premium single malt whiskies to tempt your palate.
